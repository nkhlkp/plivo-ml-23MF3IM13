My final architecture incorporates modern efficiency upgrades, replacing standard MLPs with SwiGLU blocks and LayerNorm with RMSNorm to maximize expressivity within the 2M parameter cap. To solve the Devanagari sequence length bottleneck, I implemented a custom Byte-Pair Encoding (BPE) tokenizer trained on a 256KB sample of the corpus, compressing the sequence length without hanging the CPU. I used AdamW with weight decay (exempting biases and norms), gradient clipping, and a LambdaLR cosine schedule with warmup to safely navigate the optimization landscape.
